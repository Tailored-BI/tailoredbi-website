Frequently asked questions

Does this touch my Epicor production system?
Read-only access only, during off-hours (typically 2-6am MT). Your operational system is never affected during business hours.
How does Thread install on a phone?
Thread is a Progressive Web App. On iPhone, open thread.tailored.bi in Safari, tap Share then Add to Home Screen. On Android, tap Install app. No App Store required.
Can multiple people use Thread?
Yes — Growth and Partner tiers include multi-user access with role-based modules. Each person gets their own login and Morning Thread.
What does setup take?
Typically 2-3 weeks from contract to first Morning Thread. Week 1 is infrastructure. Week 2 is data validation. Week 3 is app setup and onboarding.
