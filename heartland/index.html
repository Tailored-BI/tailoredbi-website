Company Profile
About Heartland Ag Parts

The Business

Heartland Ag Parts Co. is a mid-size make-to-stock manufacturer of agricultural replacement parts headquartered in Lohrville, Iowa. With 20+ employees across stamping, machining, welding, heat treating, powder coat, and assembly departments, Heartland supplies wear parts and service kits to ag equipment dealers and co-ops across the Corn Belt. Demand is highly seasonal - driven by planting season in spring and harvest in fall.

The Epicor Setup

Heartland runs Epicor Kinetic ERP on-premises at their Lohrville facility with a secondary distribution center in Ames. Their data spans three years of journal entries, production jobs, inventory transactions, and AR/AP cycles - all with the seasonal pattern characteristic of ag parts. The Tailored.BI warehouse connects via the SQL Server ETL lane, using SysRevChangedDate watermark-based incremental refresh.

Epicor Kinetic represents a $500K–$2M+ investment for a manufacturer like Heartland. Tailored.BI exists to make that investment visible — turning years of ERP data into the decisions that run the business.
